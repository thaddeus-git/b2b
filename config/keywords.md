# Keywords Configuration for Distributor Inspection

> **Note**: This file defines the keywords used to categorize and score potential distributors. Edit as needed.

## Target Industries (Priority Order)

### 1. Commercial Cleaning Equipment Distributors (Highest Priority)
**Products to look for:**
- Floor scrubbers, sweepers, vacuum cleaners
- Industrial cleaning machines
- Commercial cleaning equipment
- Professional cleaning machines
- Floor cleaning equipment

**Keywords (multi-language):**
- English: scrubber, sweeper, floor cleaner, vacuum, cleaning machine, commercial cleaning
- German: Scheuersaugmaschine, Kehrmaschine, Bodenreinigung, Reinigungsmaschine, Gewerbesauger
- French: nettoyeur, balayeuse, aspirateur industriel, machine de nettoyage
- Italian: spazzatrice, lavapavimenti, macchine pulizia, pulizia industriale
- Spanish: barredora, fregadora, limpiadora, máquina de limpieza

**Services to look for:**
- Technical support, maintenance, repair
- Spare parts, consumables
- Training, installation
