## Hard Gates (All Required)

| Gate | Requirement | AI Detection Method | Pass Threshold |
|------|-------------|---------------------|----------------|
| **Budget/Procurement** | Has equipment procurement/facility management function | Careers page, procurement mentions, facility management role | Procurement/facilities function identified |
| **On-site Contact** | Has facilities/operations/IT/engineering contact | Team page, leadership, facility manager role | Named contact or department |
| **Pilot Timeline** | Can launch quickly (no long renovation) | Project mentions, "quick deployment" language | No long renovation signals |

**Gate Logic:**
- **ALL PASS** → Eligible for `pilot-ready`
- **1 FAIL** → Route to `nurture`
- **2+ FAIL** → Route to `exclude`

## Value Proposition

- **Fast deployment:** 1-3 days (plug-and-play, no complex plumbing)
- **Low renovation cost:** No major infrastructure changes
- **Labor reduction:** Replace repetitive cleaning, improve consistency
- **Scalable:** Pilot first, then expand

---

## Exclusion Criteria

- No budget ownership AND no procurement path
- No on-site contact for deployment
- Research-only with no implementation timeline
