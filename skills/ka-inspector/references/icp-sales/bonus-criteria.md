# Bonus Criteria - KA End Customer Scoring

> **Status:** Bonus points for qualified KA prospects
> **Application:** Applied AFTER hard gates are evaluated
> **Total:** Capped at 100 points
> **Updated:** 2026-02-27

---

## Overview

Bonus criteria differentiate **good KA prospects** from **great KA prospects**. These are the "加分条件" from the KA ICP.

**Scoring Principles:**
- Points accumulate based on evidence strength
- Total score is capped at 100
- Hard gates must pass first for pilot-ready eligibility

---

## Base Score

| Component | Points | Condition |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Base for qualified KA prospect | 50 | Has budget + on-site contact |

---

## Bonus 1: Multi-site Potential (+30)

### ICP Rationale
"有多站点复制需求" - Companies with multiple locations can scale robot deployment faster.

### Detection Signals

| Level | Evidence | Points |
|-------|----------|--------|
| Light | 2-5 locations mentioned | +10 |
| Moderate | 6-20 locations or "chain" language | +20 |
| Strong | 20+ locations or "national chain" | +30 |

### Target Segments (Priority Order)

| Segment | Priority | Keywords |
|---------|----------|----------|
| Retail chains | Highest | "Supermarket", "retail chain", "stores" |
| Hotel chains | High | "Hotel group", "hospitality chain" |
| Property portfolios | Medium | "Property group", "real estate portfolio" |
| Office parks | Medium | "Business park", "campus" |
