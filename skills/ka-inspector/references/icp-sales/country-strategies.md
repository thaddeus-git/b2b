## Overview

---

## Priority Markets

### Hungary (HU)

**Strategic Priority:** Emerging market - growth potential

**Target Profiles:**
- Multinational subsidiaries
- Large local manufacturers
- Hotel chains in Budapest

**Market Characteristics:**
- Lower labor costs but growing shortage
- Open to automation
- EU funding for modernization

---

### Switzerland (CH)

**Strategic Priority:** High-value market - quality focus

**Target Profiles:**
- Premium hotels
- Pharmaceutical manufacturers
- Financial services (office buildings)

**Market Characteristics:**
- High labor costs (ROI favorable)
- Quality expectations
- Strict compliance requirements

---

### Greece (GR)

**Strategic Priority:** Tourism-focused market

**Target Profiles:**
- Hotel chains and resorts
- Airport facilities
- Large hospitals

**Market Characteristics:**
- Tourism-driven economy
- Seasonal labor challenges
- EU modernization funds
