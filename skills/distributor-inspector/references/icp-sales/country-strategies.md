# Country-Specific Strategies

> **Markets:** France (FR), Spain (ES), Germany (DE), Hungary (HU), Switzerland (CH), Greece (GR)
> **Updated:** 2026-02-27

---

## Overview

Each country has distinct market characteristics, distributor expectations, and sales cycles. This document provides **country-specific adjustments** to scoring and routing.

---

## France (FR)

### Strategic Priority
**Highest priority market** - Mature cleaning robot market, strong distributor ecosystem.

### Target Profile
- **Competitor distributors** (especially Pudu/Gausium) → Convert to OrionStar
- **3C/IT channels** with China brand cooperation experience
- Companies open to **RaaS/leasing models** (strong demand in France)

### Market Characteristics

| Characteristic | Implication |
|----------------|-------------|
| Lease/RaaS demand strong | Lead with flexible business model |
| Price protection sensitivity | Emphasize margin protection, MAP enforcement |
| "Exclusive" often requested | Use phased exclusivity (pilot → convert) |
| Strong competitor presence | Conversion play is primary motion |

### Scoring Adjustments

| Criterion | Adjustment |
|-----------|------------|
| Competitor footprint | **+10 bonus** (Tier 1: +100→cap, Tier 2: +70, Tier 3: +40) |
| RaaS/leasing capability | **+10 bonus** (if mentioned) |
| China brand experience | **+5 bonus** (if mentioned) |

### Detection Keywords (French)

- "Distributeur exclusif", "revendeur agréé" (authorized dealer)
- "Location", "RaaS", "leasing" (leasing model)
- "Protection des prix", "MAP" (price protection)

### Messaging Priorities

1. **Supply chain diversification** - "Add OrionStar to de-risk your China supply chain"
2. **Margin improvement** - "Better margins than [competitor] with same customer base"
3. **RaaS model support** - "We support your leasing offers with flexible terms"

## Spain (ES)

### Strategic Priority
**High priority** - Project-oriented partners, strong service culture.

### Target Profile
- **Solution providers** with project delivery capability
- Companies with **strong after-sales teams** (critical concern)
- Partners comfortable with **multi-city coverage**

### Market Characteristics

| Characteristic | Implication |
|----------------|-------------|
| Project-oriented | Partners want implementation contracts |
| After-sales sensitivity | Must address service response concerns |
| Inventory risk concern | Emphasize European warehouse, JIT replenishment |
| Relationship-driven | Longer courtship, higher trust requirements |

### Scoring Adjustments

| Criterion | Adjustment |
|-----------|------------|
| After-sales maturity | **+10 bonus** (SLA page: +25 max, spare parts: +10 max) |
| Project references | **+5 bonus** (if case studies show project delivery) |
| Multi-city coverage | **+5 bonus** (if 3+ cities mentioned) |

### Detection Keywords (Spanish)

- "Servicio postventa", "SAT" (after-sales service)
- "Proyectos", "llave en mano" (projects, turnkey)
- "Respuesta 24h", "tiempo de respuesta" (response time)
- "Almacén", "stock" (warehouse, inventory)

### Messaging Priorities

1. **Delivery speed** - "European warehouse = fast replenishment"
2. **Technical support** - "Clear SLA: 24h remote, 48h on-site"
3. **Inventory risk mitigation** - "Rolling forecast + JIT replenishment"

## Germany (DE)

### Strategic Priority
**High priority** - Quality-focused, technical evaluation culture.

### Target Profile
- **Solution providers** over pure distributors
- Companies with **technical evaluation capability**
- Partners who can handle **4-8 week testing cycles**

### Market Characteristics

| Characteristic | Implication |
|----------------|-------------|
| Rigorous testing (4-8 weeks) | Don't penalize slow conversion |
| Technical depth valued | Provide full spec sheets, compliance docs |
| Quality > price | Lead with capability, not cost |
| "Lösungsanbieter" preference | Pure resellers less trusted |

### Scoring Adjustments

| Criterion | Adjustment |
|-----------|------------|
| System integration capability | **+10 bonus** (if API/customization mentioned) |
| Technical certifications | **+5 bonus** (ISO, TÜV, etc.) |
| Test/evaluation process | **No penalty** for "evaluation required" language |

### Detection Keywords (German)

- "Lösungsanbieter", "Systemhaus" (solution provider)
- "Test", "Evaluierung", "Piloteinsatz" (test, evaluation, pilot)
- "Zertifiziert", "ISO", "TÜV" (certified)
- "Technische Spezifikationen" (technical specs)

### Messaging Priorities

1. **Technical excellence** - "Full spec sheet, compliance certifications"
2. **Testing support** - "We support 4-8 week evaluation with demo unit"
3. **German-market compliance** - "CE, TÜV, all EU certifications"

## Hungary (HU)

### Strategic Priority
**Emerging market** - Growth potential, less competitor saturation.

### Target Profile
- **First-mover advantage** partners
- Companies with **regional ambitions**
- Partners comfortable with **growth-phase collaboration**

### Market Characteristics

| Characteristic | Implication |
|----------------|-------------|
| Less competitor presence | Lower conversion urgency |
| Growth market | Partners open to building category |
| Price sensitivity | Value proposition important |
| Fewer qualified partners | Broader criteria acceptable |

### Scoring Adjustments

| Criterion | Adjustment |
|-----------|------------|
| Growth investment signals | **+10 bonus** (expansion, new hires) |
| Regional coverage | **+5 bonus** (if multi-country in CEE) |

### Detection Keywords (Hungarian)

- "Robotika", "tisztítógép" (robot, cleaning machine)
- "Növekedés", "terjeszkedés" (growth, expansion)
- "Régiós", "CEE" (regional)

## Switzerland (CH)

### Strategic Priority
**Premium market** - High-value partners, quality focus.

### Target Profile
- **Premium positioning** partners
- Companies serving **high-end facilities**
- Partners with **German/French/Italian** capability

### Market Characteristics

| Characteristic | Implication |
|----------------|-------------|
| High labor costs | ROI calculation very favorable |
| Quality expectations | Premium products expected |
| Multi-language | DE/FR/IT capability valued |
| Cross-border dynamics | Some partners operate from DE/FR border |

### Scoring Adjustments

| Criterion | Adjustment |
|-----------|------------|
| Premium positioning | **+5 bonus** (luxury hotels, high-end facilities) |
| Multi-language capability | **+5 bonus** (if DE+FR content) |

### Detection Keywords (German/French/Italian)

- "Premium", "High-End", "Luxus" (premium, high-end, luxury)
- "Mehrsprachig", "multilingue" (multi-language)
